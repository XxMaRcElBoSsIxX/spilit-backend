Your Mission

Every great AI product starts with clarity. Before you build anything, you need to answer one fundamental question:

What problem are you solving, and for whom?

The 3 Ps Framework

Use this framework to discover your idea:

  • Pain: What frustrates people? What wastes their time or money?
  • Profession: What industry or role do you understand deeply?
  • Passion: What excites you enough to work on it for years?

Your Task

Chat with Spilit to explore these three areas. Be honest and specific.
